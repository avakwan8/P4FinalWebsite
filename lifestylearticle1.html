Tips on working from home.

1. Keeping a schedule

By creating a schedule this will keep you motivated and keep you in a routine. Also, by sticking to working during your normal office hours, this will help you feel normal, as if you are at work but just in a different environment. By creating a schedule and setting boundaries for your time, this will help tremendously. Your stress levels will decrease knowing everything you need to do that day and having it all planned out. Keeping a schedule also helps you stay disciplined and stay more focused.
