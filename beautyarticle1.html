1. Paula's Choice Skin Perfecting 2% BHA Liquid Exfoliant

This cult favorite is an extremely popular product all over the world. It is a liquid exfoliant that you use after cleansing your skin. You leave this on after applying and then continue the rest of your skincare routine. This product contains salicylic acid which will help unclog your pores but is gentle at the same time. While also containing green tea, this product will make you glowy overnight!
