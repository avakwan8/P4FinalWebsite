2. Platform Boots

Platform boots have become a main staple in many young girls closet today. This style of boots channels styles from the 70's and 90's and can help you gain a few inches while also being comfortable. Today they can be both seen on the runway as well as street style. These boots can give a cool twist on any outfit and an accessory everyone should try. You could see these boots making waves on apps such as Tik Tok and Instagram where girls and brands promote and wear this style of boots everywhere.
